What is BTCsoccer?

BTCsoccer is a P2P soccer gambling website which takes Bitcoin bets placed on end-results of soccer matches. Multiple bets can be placed per player per match.

Our default & minimum stake per bet is 2mBTC (0.002BTC) but you are free to increase your stakes.

The total amount of Bitcoin wagered on a correct end result is automatically transferred and payed out to the winning bettors' Bitcoin addresses.

What does a 3% house edge mean?

A 3% commission fee is yielded on WINNING bets only. Wagers will be 100% refunded should the match NOT produce a winning bet.

What is Bitcoin and how do I get some?

Bitcoin is the first established P2P de-centralised digital currency. Bitcoin is open source and transparent: nobody owns or controls Bitcoin and everyone can take part.
See:bitcoin.org

Is it anonymous?

Bitcoin is pseudonymous as the addresses for all Bitcoin transactions are forever stored in the blockchain. As far as these addresses are linked to your identity, they will for ever be linked to you.

How does it work?

Internally each end result is a unique Bitcoin address. (Tomas)

How do I place my bets?

Select an open match from the Bets tab. LIVE matches are closed for betting as the match has already begun.

Place a bet by predicting an end result from the matches' score grid. Selected scores are added to the Bet Slip from where stakes are confirmed per bet. Upon clicking CheckOut you will we presented with a QR code and a link to process your wallet app transaction. Your bets are final when your transaction has been confirmed in the network.

How do I pay?

You pay with a Bitcoin wallet application.

How can I collect my wins?

Your winnings - minus 3% - will automatically transferred into your Bitcoin address.
